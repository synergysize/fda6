About DOGE Initiatives

The Department of Government Efficiency (DOGE) identifies and eliminates wasteful spending across federal agencies. Our mission is to improve government operations, cut unnecessary costs, and ensure taxpayer dollars are spent effectively.

This chatbot provides information about our top efficiency initiatives. You can ask questions about specific agencies, programs, savings achieved, and ongoing efforts.
